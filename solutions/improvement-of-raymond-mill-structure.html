Improvement Of Raymond Mill Structure

As traditional Raymond mill discharge fineness and low power consumption, low noise, big problems, low system efficiency of performance has been plagued by mineral processing enterprises and mill equipment manufacturers.

Raymond machine is used for grinding equipment, like barite, calcite, feldspar, talc, marble, limestone, dolomite, fluorite, lime, activated clay, activated carbon, bentonite, kaolin, cement, phosphate rock, gypsum, glass and insulation materials. In addition, Mohs hardness of less than 9.3, super fine powder processing humidity below 6% non-inflammable mineral, chemical, construction and other industries more than 280 kinds of materials.

As traditional Raymond mill discharge fineness and low power consumption, low noise, big problems, low system efficiency of performance has been plagued by mineral processing enterprises and mill equipment manufacturers. Shibang after many years of experience accumulation, the improvement on the basis of the original Raymond mill, independent research and development production of a new generation of Raymond mill, solve the technical problems of traditional Raymond mill, so as to improve the ratio of the flour mill.

The improvement of Raymond mill structure a total of four times, just as follows:

  1. The spindle and plum frame are conducive to the stability of structure transformation, keep it running smoothly.
  2. The analysis of the traditional machine part by intracavity towed, changed into outer cavity hanging transmission and cavity rotation phase isolation, so as to enhance its working stability and durability.
  3. In the crushing zone, so stay grinding material always gathered in grinding crushed zone for effective processing.
  4. Improved the grinding efficiency, at the same time also on roller structure was updated, make the roller grinding wheel and the roller can be complementary, at the same time in the rotation of the revolution, to make it stronger efficacy and durability.
  5. In ventilation section, the bellows and air duct are from raw water flat to 30 degrees to the center, form a bucket shape, ensure the bellows. Air duct and always jams, to keep the air flow and host grinding powder at high efficiency.
