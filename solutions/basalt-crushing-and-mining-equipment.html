General Introduction of Cone Crusher Used in Brazil:

Basalt are extrusive rock , volcanic eruption more relevant in the magma flow process, cooled and solidified to form , the main feature is a blowhole , rhyolite , etc., hard and tough . Intrusive rocks belong , because there is no spray the surface, close to the surface in the ground, the movement is slow, slow decline in temperature , so crystalline clear , hard and brittle. But they have in common is that both belong to the high hardness and basalt , and a higher silicon content , are more difficult to broken or crushed material in high -cost real crushing operations.

About basalt crushing and processing of materials , generally choose jaw crusher and impact crusher two-stage crushing, but with the country in recent years to improve the requirements for the type of stone tablets , the choice of three stage crushing as possible , which is coupled with the a hydraulic cone crusher, reduce flakiness . Judging from the surface of a device be more enhanced the production costs, but the long-term operation of the quarry , the broken by crushing to a high hardness and high silicon content of the three sections of stone reduced production cost is very considerable.
