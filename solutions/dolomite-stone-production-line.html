Dolomite Stone Production Line

Dolomite production line include vibrating feeder, jaw crusher, impact crusher, vibrating screen and belt conveyors and other equipment combination.

Dolomite production line include vibrating feeder, jaw crusher, impact crusher, vibrating screen and belt conveyors and other equipment combination. Depending on the process requirements, the combination of various types of equipment to meet customer requirements of different processes.

Describes the basic dolomite production line process

First, the dolomite stone from the vibrating feeder evenly sent an initial coarse crusher broken, then produced into the coarse material transported by belt conveyor to the crusher for further crushing, stone into the vibrating screen after screening different specifications of the stone, does not meet the size requirements of feed back into the stone crusher again.

dolomite production line process feature

Dolomite Stone production line high degree of automation, nesting particle size adjustable, crushing rate, energy saving, large output, producing stones uniform particle size, particle shape is good, suitable for medium and small roads and bridges and other construction projects.
