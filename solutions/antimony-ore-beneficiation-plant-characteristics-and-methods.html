Antimony Ore Beneficiation Plant Characteristics And Methods

Antimony ore processing equipment has been introduced,here explain antimony ore beneficiation methods.As almostly ore the same,the antimony ore beneficiation methods include hand-selected,gravity separation and flotation.

antimony ore hand-selected method

Antimony ore mention is the use of hand-selected antimony ore and gangue minerals color , luster , shape differences in the conduct of this beneficiation method is relatively primitive , labor-intensive , but can output high -quality chunk of antimony ore concentrate , can reduce processing costs and energy consumption. Hand-selected antimony ore is generally done in hand-selected equipment on the belt , there are many within the pit or mine mouth completed .

antimony ore gravity separation

Antimony ore processing gravity separation are applicable , because a large part of the antimony mineral density , coarse mineral and gangue ease separation method. In short , whether it is a single antimony sulfide ore or sulphide - mixed oxide antimony ore , have better conditions for re-election . And low cost of gravity separation , but also in the coarse particle size range of selected coarse concentrate a large number of qualified , discard large quantities of waste rock , thus gravity separation who are still willing antimony ore beneficiation methods used . Antimony ore gravity separation equipment mainly refers to jigger .

antimony ore flotation method

Flotation is the most important election of antimony ore beneficiation methods , antimony sulfide minerals are easy to float minerals, mostly using the flotation method to improve the ore grade . Where: stibnite often do first with lead salt activator , is also useful for copper or copper and lead salts used , then flotation collector . Common collector of butyl xanthate or shale oil and diethyldithiocarbamate mixture of pine oil frother or the 2nd oil ; antimony oxide ore is difficult to float the ore , the main use of the gravity separation sorting method .
