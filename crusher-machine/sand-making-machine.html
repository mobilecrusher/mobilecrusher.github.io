Sand Making Machine Overview

As a matter of the fact, artificial sand making machine is an ideal equipment in sand making line. SBM's B series VSI crusher and B series deep rotor vertical shaft impact crusher is the newest research result by our experienced engineers. Both of these two series crushers absorb the Germany advanced technology, and have reached world's leading standard. These two ideal artificial sand making machines can provide quality sand and gravel aggregate for mining and construction industries. Set of three kinds of crusher pattern in one, can run 720 hours continuously, Nowadays,it become the most popular equipment in sand making field.

Sand Making Processing Flow

Sand making process always includes primary crushing, fine crushing, sand making, screening, washing and other processes.

Primary crushing

We usually use jaw crusher as the primary crusher. Transport the large raw materials to jaw crusher by vibrating feeder for primary crushing.

Fine crushing

Then, send the crushed materials to cone crusher or impact crusher for fine crushing. Bely conveyor is usually used to connect production machines.

Sand making

After fine crushing, send the particles to sand making machine for further process. Then we will get uniform sand particles.

Screening

In the above processes, vibrating screen is usually used to separate the materials into different specifications.

Sand washing

If you want to get pure artificial sand, sand washing machine is necessary. After washing process, you can get pure and even sand.

This processing flow is with large output, small abrasion characteristics. And the final product is with reasonable grade. These artificial sands can be used to highway, railway, high-rise buildings, municipal, hydroelectric dam construction, and concrete mixing stations, and so on. The sand making machine is the key equipment in the processing folw production line.
