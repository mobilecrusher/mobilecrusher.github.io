Portable Crusher Overview

The portable crushing plant is a kind of high efficient crushing equipment. It adopts self-driven mode and advanced technology. This plant can move flexible and adapt the working site perfectly. This plant can be installed in a short time. Through wireless control, the portable plant can be easily driven to the trailer and be transported to the working site.Based on more than 30 years' manufacture experience, millions of equipment units' installation experience and abundant invested in research and development, SBM has released the K series portable crushing and screening plants. Here have seven kinds:coarse crushing equipment,medium and fine crushing and screening portable plant,complete portable plant,sand-making and shaping portable plant,fine crushing and sand washing portable plant,three-in-one portable plant and four-in-one portable plant.

This portable crushing plant dispels the limitation of crushing working site, environment, the complex basic configuration and the transportation of raw materials and achieves high efficiency and low investment cost. This portable crusher moves on tyre. And the chassis of the car body is high and the turning radius is small, which makes this plant convenient to move on the road especially suitable for the complex and severe road. The portable crushing plant save the time because it can run to the working site more quickly and provide more flexible working site for the whole crushing process. Besides, it saves the cost of transporting the raw materials away from the mining site.

The portable crushing plant is environmental friendly. There is noise collecting device in this plant. With reasonable structure and efficient dust collecting system, this portable crusher is ideal for construction waste recycling in urban area.
